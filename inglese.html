Welcome to Centro Remiero!

Our club was founded in 1960 and is the perfect place for rowing enthusiasts. We offer courses for beginners and advanced rowers, and regularly organize regional and national level competitions. Our goal is to promote the sport of rowing and provide a safe and welcoming environment for our members.

If you are interested in becoming a member of our club, don't hesitate to contact us!
